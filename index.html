Welcome to My Website!

With a background in Engineering (Biomechanics) and Science (Astrophysics), currently I specialize in studying neutral gas in nearby galaxies using the radio 21-cm emission line. My research focuses on nearby dwarf galaxies, exploring galaxy evolution and the impact of cluster environments on galactic dynamics mainly utilizing data from VLA and FAST, combined with multi-band analysis such as UV, Ha, and optical image.


A member of Galaxy and Cosmology Seminar, contact us if you want to join!
